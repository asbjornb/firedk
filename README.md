# Financial Independence in Denmark

Static archive of a personal finance blog focused on achieving financial independence from a Danish perspective.

**Live site:** https://firedk.net
**Original domain:** firedk.com/blog (expired)

## About

This blog covers:
- FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) basics
- Investment strategies for Danish residents
- Tax optimization (retirement accounts, dividend taxation)
- Personal finance and expense tracking

## Content

### Blog Posts (2017, 2020)
- FIRE basics and getting started
- Investment strategies and timing
- Retirement accounts vs taxable brokerage
- Buy vs rent analysis
- Dividend and tax efficiency

### Calculators
- Dividend scenarios
- Ratepension vs taxable brokerage comparison
- Simple tax estimates
- Investment frequency simulation
- Rent vs buy simulation
